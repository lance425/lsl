I'm Lance Littlefield and I build things.

What I build depends on the situation. I primarily build websites, but I have been known to build coffee tables, walls and even a pretty good relationship with my fiancée. If you are interested in building things get in touch with me somehow. Maybe we can build something together!

I'm a completely self taught developer with all of my experience coming from books, videos and articles online. I've learned quite a bit about HTML, CSS and Javascript over the past year and I can't wait to dive into server side languages like PHP and Python.

Prior to being a developer I've been a student, lineman, flooring installer, convenience store clerk, landscaper and a server. You can see from my work history I never imagined I would end up building something so abstract as websites, but here I am!

I live in Baton Rouge, LA with the aforementioned fiancée and a couple of spoiled dogs. You'll see a picture of one later! I haven't always lived in Baton Rouge. The first twenty years of my life were spent on the fast paced streets of Downsville, LA. Check it out. It's a place!
